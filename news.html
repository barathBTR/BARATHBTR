Syria crisis: Obama rejects US military intervention

President Barack Obama has described the situation in Syria as "heartbreaking" but said that unilateral US military intervention there would be a mistake. Mr Obama said President Bashar al-Assad would fall, as other dictators had fallen, but the US would try to achieve this by working to isolate Syria. More deadly clashes were reported across Syria on Tuesday. The international community remains divided on how to end violence there.

New Delhi Marathon 2023

Indian runner Man Singh won the men’s elite race at the New Delhi Marathon 2023 on Sunday with a personal best timing of two hours, 14 minutes and 13 seconds. Besides winning the top prize, Man Singh also breached the entry standard set by the Athletics Federation of India (AFI) for the upcoming Asian Games. The Asian Games entry standard for Indian men’s marathoners is 2:15:00. Man Singh was not the only Indian to achieve the Asian Games qualifying mark on Sunday.
